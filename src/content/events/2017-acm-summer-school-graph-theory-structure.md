## 📋 Program Structure

The school is organized into three weeks, each with a pair of complementary tracks:

| Week | Dates | Track A (Morning) | Track B (Afternoon) |
|------|-------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Week I** | June 26 – July 1 | Structural Graph Theory | Elementary Graph Algorithms |
| **Week II** | July 3 – July 8 | Graph Decompositions | Parameterized & Approximation Algorithms |
| **Week III** | July 10 – July 15 | Special Graph Classes | Dynamic & Approximation Algorithms |

Each weekday follows a consistent schedule:

| Time | Session |
|------|---------|
| 10:00 – 11:15 | Track A — Lecture |
| 11:15 – 11:45 | ☕ Tea/Coffee |
| 11:45 – 1:00 | Track A — Lecture |
| 1:00 – 2:00 | 🍽️ Lunch |
| 2:00 – 3:15 | Track B — Lecture |
| 3:15 – 3:45 | ☕ Tea/Coffee |
| 3:45 – 5:00 | Track B — Lecture |
| 5:00 – 7:00 | 💻 Tutorials / Labs |

Saturdays are dedicated to hands-on lab sessions.
