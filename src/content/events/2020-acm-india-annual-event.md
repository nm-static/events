## ACM-India Annual Event 2020

**February 15, 2020 — Jasubhai Auditorium, IIT Gandhinagar**

Co-located with IRISS 2020 and ACM-W Workshop on Women in Computer Science and Research.

---

## Featured Speakers

### Self-Supervised Learning: The Next Step in AI
**11:00 AM – 12:00 PM** — [Yann LeCun](http://yann.lecun.com/) (NYU, 2018 ACM Turing Award Winner)

Focus on deep learning, self-supervised learning approaches, and latent-variable models for autonomous vehicles.

### Algorithms Beyond the Worst Case
**12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** — Susanne Albers (TU München, Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz Prize)

Refined analysis approaches beyond worst-case performance evaluation.

### New Ways of Thinking of the Mobile Phone for Healthcare
**2:00 PM – 3:00 PM** — Shwetak Patel (University of Washington, 2018 ACM Prize in Computing)

Mobile device sensors for disease screening and management.

### Assistive Technology Solutions for Mobility & Education of Visually Impaired
**3:30 PM – 4:30 PM** — M. Balakrishnan (IIT Delhi, ACM Eugene L. Lawler Award)

ASSISTECH solutions including SmartCane, OnBoard, DotBook.
