## ACM-India IRISS 2020

### 14th Inter-Research-Institute Student Seminar in Computer Science

**February 13–14, 2020 — IIT Gandhinagar**

Co-located with the ACM-W Workshop (Feb 14) and ACM Annual Event (Feb 15).

---

A premier academic conference showcasing research from India's top computer science institutions, featuring keynote talks, contributed presentations, lightning talks, poster sessions, and panel discussions.
