**10:00 AM – 11:00 AM** | Speaker: Sonia Garcha (ACM India CSPathshala Committee)

An overview of the CSPathshala initiative including the Bebras Challenge, CTiS, and TACT Challenge for promoting computational thinking in schools.

---

</div>
<div class="schedule-panel" data-panel="tab-2">

**11:30 AM – 1:00 PM** | Speaker: Viraj Kumar (IISc, ACM India Education Committee Chair)

A hands-on session on using refute questions for critical code analysis. Includes practical exercises on designing and analyzing refute questions for students.

---

</div>
<div class="schedule-panel" data-panel="tab-3">

**2:00 PM – 3:30 PM** | Speaker: Venkatesh Choppella (IIIT Hyderabad)

Introduction to algorithmic program development using the Mapcode approach, with a mathematical orientation to computer science.

---

</div>
<div class="schedule-panel" data-panel="tab-4">

**4:00 PM – 5:00 PM** | Speaker: N S Kumar (Kumaradhara Consultancy)

Approaches and resources for teaching data structures effectively. Includes GitHub repository with teaching materials.
