## FSTTCS 2024

### 44th Foundations of Software Technology and Theoretical Computer Science

**December 16–18, 2024 — IIT Gandhinagar**

Workshops: December 14–15 and 19–20, 2024.

---

FSTTCS is a premier international conference in theoretical computer science, organized by IARCS and ACM India. The 44th edition features two tracks and invited speakers from leading institutions worldwide.
