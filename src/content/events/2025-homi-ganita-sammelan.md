## Ganita Sammelan 2025

**January 19–22, 2025 — IIT Gandhinagar**

A national gathering of mathematics scholars under the HoMI (History of Mathematics in India) initiative.

---

Ganita Sammelan brings together researchers, educators, and enthusiasts to explore India's rich mathematical heritage and its connections to contemporary mathematics.
