## Research Opportunities in Computer Science

**22nd March 2025 (Saturday)**

An outreach event hosted by the Department of CSE at IIT Gandhinagar.

---

Join us for *Research Opportunities in Computer Science* (ROCS) 2025, a one-day **hybrid** event showcasing research opportunities in computer science through talks, networking opportunities, and a panel discussion.

<div class="callout callout-important">
<div class="callout-title">Target Audience</div>
<div class="callout-body">

The event is designed for students, researchers, and industry professionals interested in exploring research opportunities in computer science. Whether you're considering graduate studies, looking for collaborations, or simply curious about current research trends, ROCS 2025 offers insights into the diverse landscape of computer science research.

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-info">
<div class="callout-title">Organizers</div>
<div class="callout-body">

The event is co-organized by [Nipun Batra](https://nipunbatra.github.io/), [Abhishek Bichhawat](https://iitgn.ac.in/faculty/cse/abhishek), and [Neeldhara Misra](https://www.neeldhara.com/), on behalf of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at [IIT Gandhinagar](https://iitgn.ac.in/). We are grateful to be supported by a Google Explore CSR grant and IIT Gandhinagar.

</div>
</div>
