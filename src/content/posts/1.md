## Why these pairings work

Clean sans and warm serif mixes give hierarchy without heavy styling. Pair them for headlines and body copy to keep interfaces calm.

## Quick swaps to try

- Headline: Inter SemiBold / Body: Source Sans 400  
- Headline: Playfair Display / Body: Work Sans  
- Headline: Space Grotesk / Body: Manrope  

Drop them into buttons, menus, and long-form text to see which aligns with your product voice. Switching weights alone can change the mood.***
