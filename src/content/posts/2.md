## Where to look first

Start with reputable libraries: Google Fonts for breadth, Fontshare for fresh drops, and Velvetyne for more expressive options.

## Tips before you download

- Check licenses for commercial use.  
- Test readability in small sizes and on low-contrast backgrounds.  
- Limit yourself to two families per project to keep things consistent.***
