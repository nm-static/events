## Palette ideas

- Carbon Night: charcoal, onyx, muted teal accents.  
- Noir Glow: deep navy, off-white, amber highlights.  
- Graphite: cool grays with a single neon accent.

## How to apply

Use the darkest tone for shells, a soft neutral for surfaces, and a warm accent for CTAs. Keep shadows subtle to avoid muddy depth.***
