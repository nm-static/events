## Trends showing up in production

- Warm neutrals with a single bold accent.  
- Soft micro-interactions instead of heavy parallax.  
- Rich typography with fewer decorative fonts.

## How to use them well

Focus on content density over ornamentation. Keep animations purposeful—guide attention, don’t distract. Test legibility on low-end devices.***
