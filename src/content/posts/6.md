## Quick performance wins

- Ship fewer fonts; subset where possible.  
- Lazy-load media and third-party scripts.  
- Use server-side rendering with smart caching.

## Measure, then tweak

Track Core Web Vitals, simulate slow networks, and profile hydration cost. Small changes compound—aim for consistent speed, not just great labs scores.***
